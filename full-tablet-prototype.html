Weekly Site Inspection

Conduct a thorough job site audit with this weekly site safety inspection checklist. It covers essential checks for first aid facilities, fire prevention and emergency preparedness, site security measures, personal protective equipment (PPE), housekeeping practices, workbench organization, storage and rubbish management and trip and fall safety precautions.

Annual Inventory Stocktaking

Use this inspection to count all items in your warehouse every year. It includes checks for stock accuracy, product condition, storage organisation, labelling compliance, damaged goods identification, reorder level verification, and discrepancy reporting to ensure your inventory records match physical stock.
